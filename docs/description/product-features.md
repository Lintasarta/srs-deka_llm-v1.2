# Product Features

- **AI Model Access as a Service:**  
  Deka LLM provides businesses with access to advanced Large Language Models (LLMs), enabling them to leverage AI for a variety of text-related tasks such as text generation, customer interaction, content creation, and text analysis.

- **Simplified AI Integration:**  
  The service simplifies the process of integrating AI models into business operations, enabling easy access to powerful language models without the need for businesses to manage the underlying infrastructure. This allows businesses to focus on their core operations without worrying about the complexities of AI infrastructure.

- **Token-Based Usage:**  
  The service uses a token-based model to track and limit usage, ensuring businesses can manage their resource consumption efficiently and avoid exceeding predefined usage limits.
